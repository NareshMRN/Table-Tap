Features

Everything Your Restaurant Needs

Powerful features designed to streamline operations, delight customers, and boost your revenue.

QR-Based Ordering

No app download needed — customers scan and order directly from their browser. Each table gets a unique QR code.

Real-Time Updates

Kitchen and admin notified instantly via WebSocket. Zero page refreshes — orders stream in live with sound alerts.

Live Analytics

Revenue, popular items, peak hours, and sales trends — all beautifully visualized on one powerful screen.

Digital Menu

Beautiful menu cards with images, categories, dietary filters, and search. Update prices and availability instantly.

Chef Dashboard

Simple, large-screen UI for kitchen staff. Sound notifications, order queue, and one-click status updates.

Auto Billing

Bills auto-generate when orders are marked complete. One-click printable receipts with itemized details.

Secure & Reliable

JWT-based authentication with bcrypt hashing, role-based access control, and encrypted data transmission.

Order History

Every order saved with timestamp, items, and payment status. Export reports as PDF or CSV anytime.

Customer Feedback

Star ratings and comments collected per order. Understand what your customers love and what to improve.

How It Works

From Scan to Served in 4 Steps

A seamless, frictionless experience for your customers — no app downloads, no sign-ups, no waiting.

01

Scan QR Code

Customer sits at a table and scans the unique QR code with their phone camera. No app needed.

02

Browse & Order

Browse the digital menu, add items to cart, add special notes, and place the order with one tap.

03

Kitchen Cooks

Order appears instantly on the chef's dashboard with a sound alert. Real-time status tracking begins.

04

Serve & Pay

Food is served, bill auto-generates, customer pays via cash or UPI, and rates their experience.
